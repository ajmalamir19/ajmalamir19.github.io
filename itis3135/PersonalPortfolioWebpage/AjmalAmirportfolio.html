Electricity Billing System

The Electricity Billing System is an innovative software application built using Java programming. Its primary objective is to modernize and streamline the electricity billing process. This entails precisely computing the electricity consumption over a designated period and determining the corresponding payment to be made at the electricity offices. By introducing this computerized system, it promises to enhance the billing experience for consumers, making it simpler, more accessible, and highly efficient.

Afghan KitchenConvenience website

The website I've developed is a platform dedicated to Afghan cuisine and dining, offering a range of services. It showcases experienced chefs with deep expertise in Afghan culinary traditions, providing guidance on preparing popular dishes. The emphasis is on using high-quality ingredients and exceptional cooking techniques to ensure a delightful dining experience. Users can conveniently place food orders online through the website, either by calling the business or using Facebook chat. The platform caters to larger gatherings and events, accepting orders for parties with ten or more guests, ensuring customers' dining needs are met around the clock. It offers a seamless and accessible way for individuals to explore authentic Afghan cuisine and learn the art of preparing these delightful dishes.
